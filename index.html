Our Story

Built For
Champions

APEX NYC was born in 2013 from one conviction: New York deserved a training facility that refused to compromise. Not a gym. Not a fitness center. A performance laboratory.

We built everything from the ground up — the programming, the culture, the standards. We recruited coaches who compete at the highest levels and designed spaces that demand your best.

Every detail was obsessed over. Every class was engineered. Every member who walks through our doors is treated as the elite athlete they are about to become.
