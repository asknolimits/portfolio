Our Story

A Culinary Journey
Born from Passion

Maison Noir was born from a simple belief: that exceptional food has the power to create unforgettable memories. Since 2009, we have been crafting experiences that transcend the ordinary — where each plate is a canvas and each meal is a story.

Nestled in the heart of the city, our intimate dining room seats just 42 guests — ensuring that every visit receives the attention and care it deserves. We source only the finest seasonal ingredients from local farms and trusted purveyors.

Behind the Craft

Meet Chef
Alexandre Moreau

Born in Lyon and trained at Le Cordon Bleu Paris, Chef Moreau brings over 22 years of culinary mastery to every plate. His philosophy merges classical French technique with the bold, unexpected flavors of global cuisine.

Before founding Maison Noir, Alexandre held the helm at three Michelin-starred establishments across Paris, New York, and Tokyo — each experience leaving its mark on his distinctive, evocative style.
