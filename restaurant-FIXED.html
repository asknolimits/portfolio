Our Philosophy

Where fire meets finesse

Every dish at Ember & Oak begins with a single question: what does this ingredient want to become? We source from farms within 100 miles, letting the season dictate our menu.

Our tasting menu changes weekly — a conversation between the land, the flame, and the hands of our kitchen.

Meet the Chef

"Cooking with fire is not technique. It is a dialogue — between heat, time, and the quiet patience of a great ingredient."

Marco Delacroix
Executive Chef & Co-Founder

Trained under three Michelin-starred chefs across Paris, Tokyo, and Copenhagen, Marco returned to New York with one vision: to let fire be the most honest cooking tool there is.
